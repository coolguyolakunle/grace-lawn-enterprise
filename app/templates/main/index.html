Who We Are

A First-Class Farm
Built on Integrity

Grace lawn Enterprise is a premium pig farm situated in Badagry, Lagos State, Nigeria. With a thriving herd of over 1,500 animals — including boars, sows, and piglets — we are a leading supplier of high-quality pork products.

Our major breeds include Duroc, Landrace, Large White, TN70 and Pietrain. Each bred under responsible farm management to ensure the highest quality genetics and healthiest stock year-round.
