About Us

Excellence in pig farming since day one

Welcome to

Gracelawn Enterprise

Gracelawn is a first-class pig farm located in Badagry, Lagos State, Nigeria. Our operations boast a robust herd size of over 1,500 animals, including boars, sows, and piglets. Our major breeds are Duroc, Landrace, Large White, TN70 and Pietrain.

Our farm is built on a foundation of responsible management, enabling us to maintain healthy, productive stock year-round. We produce an average of 250 stocks monthly.

What We Offer

Our Capabilities & Offerings

Large, Diverse Herd

Over 1,500 pigs in our flock with an average of 250 stocks ready for the market every month. A balanced mix of boars, sows, and piglets ensures steady availability and consistent breeding cycles.

Export Markets

Pigs for both local and international export markets. We proudly offer high-quality pig designed to meet domestic consumer needs and international standards alike.

Pure Lean Meat

Our pigs are raised to produce meat that is exceptionally lean and of quality, appealing to health-conscious customers and chefs who seek superior texture and flavor.

Best Farm Practices

We employ industry-leading farming practices focused on animal welfare, biosecurity, environmental stewardship, and traceability — delivering consistent quality with the highest standards of safety and sustainability.
